# Project 3 - PyTorch Training Laboratory

## Question

Can I build, debug, and explain a neural-network training system rather than only run a tutorial?

## Build

- Manual MLP forward/backward implementation.
- Equivalent PyTorch model and training loop.
- CNN or text-classification model.
- Configuration, seeds, checkpoints, logging, and resume support.
- Debug mode that overfits one batch.

## Experiments

- SGD vs. Adam under a fixed budget.
- Learning-rate comparison.
- Initialization or regularization ablation.
- At least three seeds for the final comparison.

## Acceptance criteria

- Curves and results are reproducible.
- Report separates optimization, generalization, and data errors.
- A deliberately broken training run is diagnosed and documented.
- Phase interview reaches 70%.
