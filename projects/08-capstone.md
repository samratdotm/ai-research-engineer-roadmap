# Project 8 - Research Engineer Capstone

## Selection rule

Choose one question at the intersection of agent evaluation, RL environments, reliable model behavior, and production systems. Extend an existing project instead of starting an unrelated application.

## Proposal must contain

- Research question and falsifiable hypothesis.
- Why the question matters.
- Related work.
- Dataset/task design.
- Baselines, treatment, controls, and metrics.
- Compute/time budget.
- Risks, confounders, and stopping rule.
- Publication and demo plan.

## Required outputs

- Reproducible public repository.
- Six-to-eight-page report.
- Model/dataset/eval cards as appropriate.
- Five-minute demo and 30-minute technical presentation.
- Two technical articles: methods and failure analysis.
- Open-source contribution related to the work.
- Resume bullets with accurate evidence.

## Final evaluation

An external engineer should be able to reproduce the affordable result, understand where it may fail, and identify what was learned beyond building the software.
