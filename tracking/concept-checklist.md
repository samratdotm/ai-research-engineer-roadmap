# Concept Mastery Checklist

Use four levels: **0 unknown**, **1 recognize**, **2 explain**, **3 implement/debug**. Target level 3 for bold concepts and level 2 for the rest.

## Foundations

- [ ] **Vectors, matrices, tensors, shapes, and broadcasting**
- [ ] **Matrix multiplication and dot products**
- [ ] Norms, projections, eigenvalues, and SVD intuition
- [ ] **Derivatives, gradients, and chain rule**
- [ ] **Expectation, variance, conditional probability, and sampling**
- [ ] **Confidence intervals, hypothesis tests, and effect size**

## Classical ML

- [ ] **Train/validation/test splits and leakage**
- [ ] **Regression, classification, regularization, and trees**
- [ ] Clustering and PCA
- [ ] **Precision, recall, F1, ROC/PR curves, and calibration**
- [ ] **Cross-validation, baselines, shift, and error slices**

## Deep learning

- [ ] **Forward pass, backpropagation, autograd, and optimizer step**
- [ ] **Activations, losses, initialization, and regularization**
- [ ] CNN and sequence-model intuition
- [ ] **PyTorch datasets, training loops, checkpoints, and debugging**
- [ ] Mixed precision and gradient accumulation

## Transformers and LLMs

- [ ] **Tokenization, embeddings, attention, masking, and transformer blocks**
- [ ] **Causal LM objective and next-token generation**
- [ ] **Pre-training, SFT, LoRA, quantization, and decoding**
- [ ] Context windows, KV cache, batching, latency, and throughput
- [ ] Data curation, contamination, and model cards

## Evaluations

- [ ] **Task distribution, success criteria, baselines, and controls**
- [ ] **Deterministic, human, and LLM grading**
- [ ] **Judge calibration, agreement, and bias**
- [ ] **Seeds, confidence intervals, ablations, and error analysis**
- [ ] **Agent trajectories, OOD evaluation, and reward hacking**

## RL and post-training

- [ ] **MDPs, policies, trajectories, returns, and value functions**
- [ ] Q-learning, policy gradients, actor-critic, and PPO
- [ ] **SFT, reward modeling, RLHF/RLAIF, DPO, and GRPO**
- [ ] **On/off-policy, KL control, reward shaping, and reward hacking**

## ML systems

- [ ] GPU memory/compute/bandwidth intuition
- [ ] **DDP, FSDP, tensor/pipeline parallelism tradeoffs**
- [ ] **Checkpointing, lineage, fault recovery, and observability**
- [ ] **Continuous batching, KV cache, quantization, and serving metrics**
